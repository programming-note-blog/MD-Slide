Privacy Policy

App: MD Slide ｜ Last updated: March 31, 2026

programming-note-blog ("the Developer") respects the privacy of users of the MD Slide application ("the App") and is committed to protecting personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled within the App.

1. Information Collected by the App

The App itself does not directly collect or transmit any personal information. All data — including Markdown text, images, and exported files — is stored only on your device and is never sent to external servers.

However, the App uses a third-party advertising service that may collect certain information, as described below.

2. Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data on your device for usability purposes:

  • Last opened file bookmark: Used to automatically reopen your file the next time the App is launched.
  • Imported images: Saved in the App's Documents folder for insertion into Markdown documents.
  • Temporary files: Generated temporarily during PDF export and automatically deleted after export is complete.

None of this data is transmitted externally.

3. Advertising Service (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob, provided by Google LLC, for advertising. AdMob may automatically collect the following information for ad delivery and personalization:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • Device information (model name, OS version, etc.)
  • IP address (used to estimate approximate location)
  • App usage data (ad impressions, clicks, etc.)
  • Crash logs and diagnostic information

This information is handled in accordance with Google's privacy policies. For details, please see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob & AdSense Policies

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The App itself does not use cookies. However, AdMob may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to optimize ad delivery.

You can limit personalized advertising by disabling "Allow Apps to Request to Track" in your iOS Settings.

5. Sharing Information with Third Parties

The Developer does not sell, share, or provide users' personal information to any third party, except for the information collected by AdMob as described above.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. The Developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If it is discovered that personal information has been collected from a child under 13, the Developer will take prompt steps to delete such information.

7. Security

The Developer takes reasonable technical measures to protect user information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When changes are made, the last updated date on this page will be revised. For significant changes, a notification may be provided within the App. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

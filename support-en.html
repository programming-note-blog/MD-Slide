Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. What file formats does the app support?

The app supports reading and saving Markdown files (.md). You can also export slides as PDF.

Q. What is Marp?

Marp is a framework for generating presentation slides from Markdown. By using special directives (such as --- for slide breaks and marp: true declarations), you can create beautiful slides from plain Markdown text.

Q. How can I share my slides?

Use the PDF export feature to convert your slides to a PDF file, then share via email, AirDrop, cloud storage, or other methods.

Q. Are Mermaid diagrams supported?

Yes. You can render flowcharts, sequence diagrams, Gantt charts, and more using Mermaid syntax within your slides.

Q. Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. All data is stored only on your device. No data is ever sent to external servers.

Q. Can I remove ads?

An ad-free option is not currently available. We may consider this in a future update.
